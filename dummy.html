Workshop on Career Roadmap by
Mr. Veeru Singh Sokhi (CEO - Global Career Councilling)

Sunday, Oct 18th at 4 pm

This workshop will focus on the concept of ‘Career Readiness’. Students will be introduced to real-world practical activities that aid in clarifying their career direction. This workshop will provide an insight into how to write a cover letter, offer an introduction into the basics of resume writing and encourage students to begin thinking about their ‘personal branding’ and how this can influence their career pathway.
